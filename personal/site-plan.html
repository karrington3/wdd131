D&D Character and NPC Tracker

The purpose of this website is to serve as an all-in-one tool for Dungeon Masters (DMs) to track campaign information, including player characters, NPCs, and world settings, with features for organizing stats, abilities, and loot. It aims to streamline session management and enhance gameplay by providing quick access to essential data during sessions.

Audience: The intended audience includes Dungeon Masters who run Dungeons & Dragons campaigns and are looking for an efficient way to manage game details, keep track of characters and encounters, and streamline combat with an initiative tracker.
